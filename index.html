About Me

Hey there, I'm Alex.


I'm a total fitness and nutrition nut (I've got a first class honours degree in Sport and Exercise Science and a distinction in Human Nutrition at Master's level to prove it!). As a registered nutritionist, I'm all about making evidence-based content accessible and easy to understand for everyone.


But I'm not just about the science-y stuff - I'm also obsessed with all things health, wellbeing, and mindset. From trying out new wellness trends to practicing yoga to reading up on the latest self-care tips, I believe that taking care of your body and mind is the key to living your best life.


So if you're looking for a fun, laid-back, and informative resource to help you on your health journey, you've come to the right place.


Let me show you what I can do for you!

How I Can Help

Bronze Plan

  • 30-minute initial consultation with the Registered Dietitian
  • Basic dietary recommendations based on the client's health goals and needs
  • Weekly check-ins via email or phone for one month

Silver Plan

  • 45-minute initial consultation with the Registered Dietitian
  • Customized meal plan based on the client's health goals and needs
  • Bi-weekly check-ins via email or phone for two months
  • Access to a recipe database with healthy meal options

Gold Plan

  • 60-minute initial consultation with the Registered Dietitian
  • Customized meal plan based on the client's health goals and needs
  • Weekly check-ins via email or phone for three months
  • Access to a recipe database with healthy meal options
  • Supplement recommendations, if needed

Ultima Plan

  • 90-minute initial consultation with the Registered Dietitian
  • Customized meal plan based on the client's health goals and needs
  • Weekly check-ins via email or phone for six months
  • Access to a recipe database with healthy meal options
  • Supplement recommendations, if needed
  • In-person or virtual grocery store tour to help the client make healthy choices while shopping
